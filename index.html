Technology and competitively

Asset management within EAM Enterprise involves recording, maintaining, structuring, and standardizing asset information comprehensively. It captures details such as the identity, configuration, and structure of physical assets, encompassing both technical and commercial configurations. This includes tracking the current position, whether by location, functional position, or tag and also maintaining records of past locations and the complete maintenance history of each asset.

Materials Management

EAM Enterprise excels in materials management by assessing and establishing appropriate stocking levels to ensure a satisfactory service level for parts and supplies, meeting anticipated maintenance demands. It focuses on minimizing the working capital allocated to stocked parts and supplies while streamlining operations through automated processes. These include managing storage rooms, handling purchase requisitions, goods receipts, returns, parts repairs, and maintaining meticulous record-keeping, all contributing to efficient and effective materials management.

Procurement Management

EAM Enterprise covers the entire procurement cycle seamlessly, overseeing requisition creation, approvals, supplier selection, purchase order placement, goods receipt, and invoice matching. It ensures a streamlined process from selecting qualified suppliers to obtaining the most competitive prices. This comprehensive approach to procurement management facilitates efficient operations and cost-effective purchasing, optimizing every step of the procurement process.

Work Management

EAM Enterprise offers robust work management capabilities by efficiently overseeing, planning, and monitoring work processes along with the necessary resources for completion. It facilitates the creation of standard, regular, and preventive maintenance work orders and schedules. Additionally, it allows for defining comprehensive work information such as supervisors, permits, employee qualifications, shifts, tools, checklists, and various other relevant details. This ensures a structured and organized approach to managing work tasks efficiently.

Project Management

EAM Enterprise streamlines project management by automating the administration of the entire project lifecycle. It encompasses initial budgeting, time-scale planning, and oversees the project until its final completion. The platform facilitates the comparison between actual progress, work status, resource utilization, and costs against the initial project plan. This capability provides comprehensive insights, enabling effective monitoring and control throughout the project's duration, ensuring alignment with planned objectives and budgets.

EAM Mobile

HxGN EAM Mobile ensures seamless transitions for workers moving between connected and disconnected environments, guaranteeing consistent application performance regardless of the connectivity status. It empowers workers to access, capture, and manage information directly from job sites. This capability significantly enhances productivity and facilitates more effective decision-making by providing on-site access to essential data and tools without concerns about connectivity issues affecting performance.
